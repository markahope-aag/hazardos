**Regulatory Fees:** Yes (DHS/DNR)

## Scope Statement

We hereby submit specifications and estimates for the removal of asbestos-containing materials.

## Work Practices

- Regulate the area via banner tape and signs
- Cover the ground with 6-mil plastic
- Use of wet methods and prompt cleanup during removal
- Stage a decontamination area next to the regulated area
- Use of HEPA vacuums, as applicable
- Use of asbestos-labeled bags to dispose of the asbestos waste in an appropriate landfill by a third-party hauler
- Workers to wear appropriate Personal Protective Equipment
