# Miscellaneous Asbestos Removal

**Proposal Title:** Asbestos Removal Proposal
**Hazard Category:** Asbestos
**Regulatory Fees:** Yes (DHS/DNR)
**Default Payment Terms:** 30 days

## Scope Statement

We hereby submit specifications and estimates for the removal of asbestos-containing materials as listed below.

## Work Practices

- Put up critical barriers (plastic) and signs to regulate the area
- Set up decontamination units, as necessary
- Seal all HVAC vents inside the regulated area (if any inside of it)
- Set up air scrubbers with HEPA filtration units to establish pressure differential (as applicable)
- Use of asbestos-labeled bags to dispose of the asbestos waste in an appropriate landfill by a third-party hauler
- Apply a lockdown encapsulant once the abatement process is done
- **If floor tile adhesive to be removed is on concrete, we will use Sentinel 805 Envirowash to wash it.** Sentinel 805 Envirowash is a concentrated product specifically formulated for final preparation of subfloors following mastic removal on asbestos jobs and lead dust cleanup
- Workers wearing appropriate personal protection equipment
