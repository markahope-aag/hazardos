# Encapsulation (TSI)

**Proposal Title:** Abatement Proposal
**Hazard Category:** Asbestos
**Regulatory Fees:** Yes (DHS/DNR)
**Default Payment Terms:** 15 days

## Scope Statement

We hereby submit specifications and estimates for the removal of asbestos-containing materials.

## Work Practices

- Put up critical barriers (plastic) and/or banner tape with signs to regulate the area
- Seal all HVAC vents inside the regulated area
- Use of the glovebag procedure to remove the TSI
- Use of wet methods and prompt cleanup during removal
- Set up air scrubbers with HEPA filtration units to establish pressure differential (if applicable)
- Set up decontamination units
- Use of HEPA vacuums
- Use of asbestos-labeled bags to dispose of the TSI in an appropriate landfill by a third-party hauler
- We will use AK-CRYL CP-9 to encapsulate the TSI
