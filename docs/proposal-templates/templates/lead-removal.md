**Proposal Title:** Lead Safe Renovation Demo Proposal
**Hazard Category:** Lead
**Regulatory Fees:** No

## Scope Statement

We hereby submit specifications and estimates for the removal of lead-containing materials.

## Work Practices

- Containing dust inside the work area by building an enclosure with HEPA filtration units (interior work only and as applicable)
- Cover the ground with 6-mil plastic (as applicable for interior and/or exterior work)
- Workers wearing appropriate PPE
- Set up a worker's decontamination area
- Using dust-minimizing work methods such as wetting the material via airless sprayer and/or Hudson sprayer
- Conducting prompt cleanup during the removal
- Bag or wrap material for disposal
